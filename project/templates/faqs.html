Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the Wellness Tracker?

The Wellness Tracker is a web-based application that allows users to input their recorded health data. It then analyzes this data and provides insights to help you monitor your fitness progress.

2. How do I upload my health data?

You can upload your health data by going to the "Upload Data" section, where you'll enter details such as steps taken, calories burnt, heart rate, and other relevant health metrics.

3. How does the tracker help me visualize my progress?

The Wellness Tracker uses data analysis and visualization tools to create reports, graphs, and charts, showing your fitness progress over time. You can track metrics like daily steps, activity levels, and calories burned.

4. Can I track my sleep patterns?

Currently, the Wellness Tracker focuses on physical activities such as steps, calories, and heart rate. Sleep tracking may be added in future updates.

5. How secure is my data?

Your privacy is important to us. We use state-of-the-art encryption to ensure that your personal health data is securely stored and protected from unauthorized access.
