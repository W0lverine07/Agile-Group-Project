Track Your Health, Your Way

Our wellness tracker lets you log your daily health and fitness data all in one place. From steps taken and calories burned to sleep hours and heart rate, you stay in control by manually entering what matters most to you. It's perfect for people who want to monitor their progress without needing a fitness watch or wearable device.

Simple, Personal, and Private

Create a profile with just the basics—your age, height, weight, and other key info—to get started. You can even upload a profile photo. Once you're set up, you can add daily updates like your activity type, workout time, or rest patterns. All your data stays with you, helping you reflect on your habits and build a healthier routine over time.

See Your Progress Visually

After entering your data, you’ll be able to view clean, easy-to-read visuals that show how you're doing. Whether you’re tracking for fitness, recovery, or general wellness, our app gives you the tools to spot trends and stay motivated. Everything is designed to work smoothly on all screen sizes—from desktops to phones.
